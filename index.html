Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Data Architect based in the UK, with close to two decades of experience designing and building data platforms that turn complex, messy data into something organisations can actually trust and act on.

My core expertise is in building Data Lake, Delta Lake, and Lakehouse architectures on AWS and Azure — the kind of foundations that power advanced analytics, AI, and machine learning workloads at scale. I'm certified across AWS, Databricks, and Snowflake, and I bring hands-on depth in Spark, Databricks, dbt, EMR, AWS Glue, Hadoop, Hive, SQL, Python, and PySpark.

Most recently, I led the build of an enterprise-grade Lakehouse on Databricks/Azure for one of the UK's largest building societies — owning the data strategy roadmap, tooling decisions, and a cross-functional engineering and BI team. Earlier in my career, I worked on clinical trial and drug development data at Novartis, and led data engineering for a major telecom and energy client base at Cognizant.

Beyond the platform work, I've ventured into GenAI — building a RAG and vector-search based solution on Databricks for internal knowledge access, and deploying ML models (logistic regression, XGBoost) on AWS SageMaker. I also care about doing this properly: DevOps, CI/CD, code quality (SonarQube, lint tooling), and proper governance are part of how I work, not an afterthought.
